=== Recipe 1: Classic Apple Pie ===
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Peel, core, and slice 6 medium Granny Smith apples.
3. In a bowl, toss the apples with 3/4 cup sugar, 1 tsp cinnamon, and 1 tbsp lemon juice.
4. Line a 9-inch pie dish with dough, fill with the apple mixture, and cover with a second crust.
5. Bake for 50 minutes until golden brown.

=== Recipe 2: Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken ===
1. In a bowl, mix 1/4 cup olive oil, juice of 2 lemons, 2 cloves minced garlic, and 1 tbsp dried oregano.
2. Marinate 4 chicken breasts in the mixture for 30 minutes.
3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
4. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side until the internal temperature reaches 165°F.
5. Serve immediately with fresh parsley.

=== Recipe 3: Simple Tomato Basil Soup ===
1. Sauté 1 chopped onion and 2 minced garlic cloves in 2 tbsp olive oil until translucent.
2. Pour in 2 cans (28 oz each) of crushed tomatoes and 2 cups of vegetable broth.
3. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 20 minutes.
4. Blend the soup until smooth using an immersion blender.
5. Stir in 1/2 cup fresh shredded basil and 1/4 cup heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper.

=== Recipe 4: Perfect Scrambled Eggs ===
1. Crack 4 eggs into a bowl and whisk vigorously until frothy.
2. Melt 1 tbsp of butter in a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat.
3. Pour in the eggs and let them sit for 30 seconds until the edges start to set.
4. Gently fold the eggs using a silicone spatula, pushing them towards the center.
5. Remove from heat while they are still slightly runny. They will finish cooking on the plate.

=== Recipe 5: Quick Beef Stir-Fry ===
1. Slice 1 lb flank steak diagonally against the grain.
2. Mix 1/3 cup soy sauce, 1 tbsp brown sugar, and 1 tsp sesame oil for the sauce.
3. Heat 1 tbsp vegetable oil in a wok over high heat. Sear the beef for 2 minutes, then remove.
4. Add 2 cups of broccoli florets and 1 sliced bell pepper to the wok. Stir-fry for 3 minutes.
5. Return the beef to the wok, pour in the sauce, and toss until everything is coated and warm.

=== Recipe 6: Three-Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies ===
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a medium bowl, combine 1 cup of creamy peanut butter, 1 cup of granulated white sugar, and 1 large egg.
3. Mix until the dough is smooth and uniform.
4. Roll into 1-inch balls, place on a baking sheet, and press down with a fork to form a crisscross pattern.
5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the edges are golden.

=== Recipe 7: Garlic Parmesan Roasted Asparagus ===
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Trim the tough ends off 1 bunch of asparagus spears.
3. Toss the asparagus with 1 tbsp olive oil, 2 cloves minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
4. Spread in a single layer on a baking sheet and bake for 12 minutes.
5. Remove from the oven, sprinkle immediately with 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, and serve.

=== Recipe 8: Classic Spaghetti Carbonara ===
1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook 12 oz spaghetti until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
2. Meanwhile, fry 4 oz diced pancetta in a large skillet until crispy.
3. In a bowl, whisk together 3 large eggs, 1 cup freshly grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper.
4. Add the hot, drained pasta to the skillet with the pancetta off the heat.
5. Quickly toss the pasta with the egg mixture, adding reserved pasta water until a creamy sauce forms.

=== Recipe 9: Fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes ===
1. Whisk 2 cups all-purpose flour, 3 tbsp sugar, 1.5 tsp baking powder, 1.5 tsp baking soda, and 1 tsp salt.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk 2.5 cups buttermilk, 2 large eggs, and 1/3 cup melted butter.
3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Stir until just combined (lumps are okay).
4. Heat a buttered griddle over medium heat. Pour 1/3 cup batter for each pancake.
5. Flip when bubbles form on the surface, and cook until golden brown.

=== Recipe 10: Easy Caprese Salad ===
1. Slice 3 large, ripe heirloom tomatoes into 1/4-inch thick rounds.
2. Slice 1 lb fresh mozzarella cheese into exactly the same thickness.
3. On a large platter, alternate layers of tomato and mozzarella slices.
4. Tuck whole fresh basil leaves between each layer.
5. Drizzle generously with extra-virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze. Sprinkle with flaky sea salt.
